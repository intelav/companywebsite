Embedded Research & Application

Dedicated prototypes and experiments focused on optimizing AI, Computer Vision, and LLMs for edge devices and mobile platforms (Android/Jetson).

Multi-Barcode Detection (Real-time CV)

Real-time Edge Vision for Inventory & Retail

This prototype focuses purely on **Optimized Object Detection** on mobile hardware, enabling the device to simultaneously recognize and track multiple barcodes or product items with low latency. This core computer vision work is foundational for fast, intelligent retail applications.

**Key Technologies:** Android NDK/Kotlin, Optimized YOLO/SSD models, Fast Inference Engines.

AR Engagement Use Case (Augmented Reality)

Blending Digital Information with the Physical World

This demonstration showcases how **Augmented Reality (AR)** can be layered onto computer vision results. Once an item is detected (using the technology from the Multi-Barcode Project), ARCore is used to overlay digital information, directions, or interactive 3D models onto the real-world view.

**Key Technologies:** ARCore, Android SDK, Real-time Visual Overlays.

Voice LLM on Edge Devices

Running Generative AI Models Locally

A prototype exploring the deployment of compact **Voice/Speech-to-Text** and **Large Language Models (LLMs)** directly on edge hardware. This research focuses on quantization techniques and accelerated inference engines to achieve low-latency, privacy-preserving conversational AI without relying on cloud services.

**Key Technologies:** Optimized LLM variants (e.g., Llama.cpp/Whisper), Android/Embedded C++ deployment, Quantization.

SmartPurchase App (Archived Experiment)

Retail AI & Personalized Recommendation System

An early full-stack prototype demonstrating how Computer Vision and Generative AI can transform the shopping experience. Features included real-time personalized discounts, AI-driven shop management tools, and enhanced product exploration based on user behavior and camera input.

**Status:** Archived/Proof-of-Concept. Insights inform current edge AI optimization work.
