Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical AI research and real-world deployment, particularly in the realm of embedded and edge computing. We specialize in optimizing complex AI models for real-time, power-efficient operation.

We operate as an independent lab, allowing us to pursue innovative, long-term projects without the constraints of typical product development cycles.
